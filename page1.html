PETAUKE DISTRICT HEALTH OFFICE


Petauke district health office is located right in the heart of petauke district,its a small town with activities that improves its
development so fast.It is situated off the east road heading to chipata,from off the road it only take about 2km to reach the town center.
As a district health office it is composed of 30 health facility fully functioning inclusive hospitals of levels and min hospitals.
And in the province it is one of the district that is providing early child development services (ECD) together with Ketete district health.
The government of zambia is committed t0 ensuring that all families are suported to care for their children and help them not only to survive but to
thrive ana develop their full potential.With the current level of stunting growing coupled with ohter risk factors.

it is kown fact that most of the children under five years in zambia fail to reach appropriate developmental milestones.
All parents or guidian fully understands that failure to reach apprpriate develpmental milestones in the early years often is expensive to reverse
in later life.
It is understood that all zambians should be provided with equity acces to quality health services as close to the family as ppossible.In this regard
the government through the sector ministry of health provides health education to caregivers on the early child development importance as early as possible.
Parents are taken through various lessons as far as during pregnancy how they need to be conducting themselves for the healthness of the baby starting from the womb
to birth.When the child is born parents are taught how to bring up a child in a health way through following right steps and providing right feeding according to the
age bands.


Community based personel called CBVs helps alot and play a major role in diserminating ECD messages to communities,therefore this helps to move forwad the
achieving of intended goals and taking services closer to the people.Building CBVs in educating parents on Early Child Development will assist in bringing the programme
activities closer to caregivers and families in order to promote good and appropriate caregivers and families knowledge and practice that will assist children survive,thrive,
and develpment their full potential.
